Cadejo Manual









caeli combo




Combo is a simple electronic organ with 4 tones elements, each with associated mix and wave slider.  Elements 1 and 2 produce pulses waves while elements 3 and 4 use FM feedback to produce sine, Fm ''sawtooth" and  FM noise.

With the coarse and fine detune sliders at 0 the frequency relationship between the 4 elements is 1:2:3:4. With the coarse slider at 1.0 the frequency relationship becomes 1:3:4:5, Intermediate positions produce non-harmonic tones. 


The tone may be further modified by the key-tracking filter.  The filter modes are: Bypass, Lowpass,  Highpass,  Bandpass  and  Notch. 

The number box below the filter curve sets the cutoff/center
in terms of harmonic number.  For example if the key A440 is played
and the filter is set to the 4th harmonic the cutoff  is 1760 Hz.


Vibrato is introduced by the Modulation wheel and the two vibrato sliders are rate and sensitivity. The vibrato rate has an approximate range between  0.17 to 7 Hz.

The Flanger has four controls, Rate, Feedback, Depth and Mix. The
Rate and Feedback values are set by moving the 'x' handle within the
rectangular field.

The Reverb sets the level of the wet reverb signal. The reverb decay time is fixed.

All amplitude and mix sliders have linear responses.
